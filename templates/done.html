Study Debrief

In this study, you interacted with two different AI tutor designs:

  • Standard Tutor: A conventional AI tutor designed to explain concepts clearly and guide problem-solving.
  • Future-Self Tutor: An AI tutor framed as your own future self — someone who has already mastered the subject and is looking back to guide you. It was prompted to share memories of struggling with these same concepts.

We are investigating whether this identity-based framing changes how motivated and supported students feel during learning. Your honest responses are exactly what makes this research meaningful.
